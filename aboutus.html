Terafina provides next-generation, cloud-based sales solutions for mid-market financial institutions. The company was founded by industry veterans with strategic and operational management experience in Digital Banking and Technology.

Terafina is built on best practices developed at the country’s largest banks, Its solutions empower a financial institution’s customers and employees to interact seamlessly at all phases and stages of the sales cycle, regardless of product, channel, or digital device. Our commitment is to continuously innovate by adopting new technology.
Management Team
Meheriar Hasan, CEO/Co-Founder
Mr. Hasan, principle founder and CEO, is a proven thought leader and seasoned operator in Digital Banking space in the U.S., brings 27 years of hands-on experience building the North America’s largest direct/digital distribution channels to generate new profit sources for consumer and commercial banks, insurance, and consumer finance companies.

Mr. Hasan is frequent keynote speaker, and author of numerous articles on direct/digital Banking and widely quoted in global publications. Mr. Hasan holds a Master of Science in Finance and a Bachelor of Arts in Economics with a minor in Mathematics from the University of Arizona.

Boris Grinshpun, VP Sales & Marketing/Co-Founder
Mr. Grinshpun has deep experience in Professional Services with F500 firms, including 15 years in strategy consulting and consumer banking, delivering market growth/acquisition and portfolio management strategies. He has led many CRM Implementations, and specializes in data, analytics, and digital marketing. His resume includes executive positions at US Bank and Wells Fargo in San Francisco.

Mr. Grinshpun holds a Bachelors of Business Administration in Management Information Systems from the University of Michigan.

Tiffani Montez, VP Support and Implementation
Ms. Montez has deep experience in global Financial Services with over 20 years of experience in strategic planning, strategic execution, and business process redesign. Her resume includes executive positions at Wells Fargo Bank and most recently, she was a Principal Analyst at Forrester Research.

Ms. Montez has been a guest speaker at Net.Finance and the Consumer Banker Association, among other industry conferences. While at Forrester Research, she wrote research on topics such as digital financial sales, multichannel banking, mobile banking, and personal financial management. She has been quoted in Forbes and American Banker on the future of mobile banking.
