Start with any module and add as you go, or implement all at once.
Terafina-Digital
Delivers a device aware sales platform directly to your customers. The intuitive flow and design guides your customers and prospects through the application process seamlessly, while increasing cross-sell and improving digital sales conversions.
Terafina-Call Center
Call Center integration is crucial for remote customers. Terafina Call Center integrates with Digital Sales, providing a seamless sales process and giving agents the ability to better understand and respond to customers' sales requests in real-time. In addition Terafina Call Center is designed to improve sales agent productivity by delivering prioritized digital leads and referrals in Real-Time to your staff.
Terafina-Branch
Terafina Branch integrates digital sales capability with physical branch locations, significantly increasing the ability of branch employees to engage in high-touch, high-value sales interactions with their customers. Terafina Branch allows financial institutions to manage and optimize employee effectiveness through built-in analytics and real-time monitoring of agents' sales process across your organization.
